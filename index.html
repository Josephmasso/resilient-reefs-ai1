About Accentecx

Accentecx is a 60% Filipino-owned, Iloilo-based AI data annotation and evaluation company, dedicated to fostering a student-led workforce development hub in partnership with the University of the Philippines Visayas. Our unique strategy focuses on critical sectors: marine, agriculture, and responsible AI, with future expansion into defense. We believe in harnessing local talent to build global AI solutions for a more sustainable and secure future.

Legal & Compliance

Accentecx is structured as a 60% Filipino-owned, 40% foreign-owned domestic stock corporation. This ensures compliance with Philippine foreign ownership laws. All operations adhere to the Revised Corporation Code of the Philippines and relevant government regulations. Intellectual Property (IP) generated from commercial projects will belong to our clients, while IP from open-source initiatives will be clearly licensed for public benefit. Our team's roles are meticulously defined to avoid conflicts of interest and ensure full legal compliance.
